His Life - The Unique Man

Steve Jobs meets Steve Wozniak, 5 years his elder, through a mutual friend. Woz and Steve share a love of electronics.Apple Computer Inc. is incorporated by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ron Wayne.

1 APR 1976

Apple's board sides with John Sculley and strips Steve off all executive duties.Steve Jobs resigns from Apple and starts NeXT with five other refugees from Apple. Apple announces it will sue NeXT.

May 1985

Gil Amelio is ousted by the Apple Board of directors after a disastrous quarter. Steve Jobs is named interim CEO in his place and installs his NeXT executive team at the top of Apple.

JUL 1997

A Thousand Songs. In Your Pocket After an 8-month crash development program, Steve Jobs unveils iPod at a small media event on the company's campus. He has no idea how it will tranform Apple.

23 Oct 2001

Apple makes a huge sensation at the West Coast Computer Faire with a prototype Apple II.The Apple II becomes the first mass-market personal computer.

17 Apr 1977

Apple, which was desperately looking for a modern operating system to buy, eventually buys NeXT for $400 million. Steve Jobs is named "informal adviser" to Apple CEO Gil Amelio

Dec 1996

Say hello to iMac Steve Jobs introduces Apple's revolutionary iMac at the Flint Center auditorium in Cupertino, 14 years after he had introduced the Macintosh at that same place.

6 May 1998

Welcome to iPhone! In his most memorable keynote presentation ever, at Macworld 2007, Steve Jobs introduces iPhone and its revolutionary touch-screen interface. He also introduces Apple TV and announces the company's name change from Apple Computer Inc. to Apple Inc. to better reflect its new nature.

9 Jan 2007
